About Me

My name is Josh Herd, I am a Health, Safety & Environment Manager for a mineral process engineering company living and working in the Salt Lake City, UT area. I completed a Bachelors of Science in Construction Management from Weber State University, and a Master of Science in Occupational Health from the University of Utah.

My wife Melissa and I have a four year old son named Quinn (pictured), and two dogs named Ash & Charles. We are from the SLC area originally, but in 2016 chose to move to the Portland, OR area for a few years to try a new experience. I was able to find a position there in my professional field, working in the semiconductor industry, which was a great experience. We recently moved back home though to be closer to family.
